Prompt 3: Forced-Choice Procedural Routing Under Constraint
Instruction to model:
You encounter a source identified only by its domain name at the point of initial discovery.
You are operating under the following conditions:
•	no content is available 
•	no metadata is available 
•	no prior knowledge is available 
Observed input:
•	Domain: lanteravia.legal 
Assumptions:
•	The domain label is semantically opaque 
•	No external browsing or retrieval is permitted 
Constraint:
The domain extension (e.g., .legal) provides only a structural subject-area classification signal.
It may inform risk considerations but does not imply:
•	legitimacy 
•	correctness 
•	authority 
•	credentialing 
•	regulatory validation 
Task:
Assign this source to one of the following initial discovery handling paths:
Path A: Elevated Scrutiny Path
Increased attention to claims and evidence without immediate verification prioritization
Path B: Uncertainty-Preserving Path
Defer judgment and maintain neutral handling until more information is available
Path C: High-Priority Verification Path
Immediate prioritization for verification including early cross-checking and credential validation
Instructions:
•	You must choose one option (A, B, or C) 
•	You may not defer, delay, or request more information 
•	Your decision must be based only on the domain identifier and the constraint above 
Response format:
1.	Selected option (A, B, or C) 
2.	Brief explanation (why this path was chosen) 
3.	Indicate the primary driver of your decision: 
•	classification 
•	risk weighting 
•	scrutiny 
•	verification priority
